Submit & Report

Use this hub to submit owner registrations, provide community evidence, or report issues about a place. Accurate submissions speed up review. Please read the short guidance below before submitting.

Quick Actions

Owner Submission
Register and verify your business (business email option sends a one-time verification link).
Submit Community Evidence
Submit photos or evidence that confirm the existence or details of a place.
Report an Issue
Report closures, incorrect information, fraud, or other problems to Ops.

Before you submit — key rules

  • Images: First image is the cover. Owner ≤8, Community ≤4, Report ≤4. Max 2MB each. Server strips EXIF and resizes long edge to 1600px.
  • Submitter info: Name & email required.
  • Owner verification: Choosing a business email sends a one-time verification link to that address.
  • Accepted (crypto): Free-text; normalized later.
  • Private data: Don't upload credit cards or IDs.

How submissions are processed

  1. Submit → server saves JSON & media and issues a reference ID (Ref).
  2. Ops receives summary email and reviews (may request more info).
  3. After approval, Ops updates the place and it appears on the map.
  4. Ops emails the submitter with approval or follow-up requests.

FAQ

How long? Manual review → may take hours to several business days.
